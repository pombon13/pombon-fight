📖 Complete Game Guide

How to Play

Everything you need to know to survive the waves, collect power-ups, and beat your high score.

Controls

Dead Simple. Just Point.

🖱️
Mouse (Desktop)
Move your cursor anywhere on screen. Pombon moves toward it and auto-aims at the nearest enemy.
👆
Touch (Mobile/Tablet)
Drag your finger around the screen. Pombon follows your touch. Works on any touchscreen.
🔫
Auto-Firing
No tap or click needed — Pombon shoots automatically toward your cursor whenever enemies are present.
💊
Collecting Power-Ups
Move Pombon over a glowing orb on the ground to pick it up. Walk into it — no button needed.

Wave Progression

What to Expect

WaveWhat Happens
1–2Just Ghosts. Use this to learn the controls and collect your first power-ups.
3–4Speeders join — they're fast, don't let them sneak past. Zigzaggers are tricky.
5⚠️ First Boss wave! Dragons and Tanks also join the regular pool.
6–6Mixed waves — expect Dragons, Ice Spirits and Tanks all at once.
7–9Shooters fire back at you. Splitters create mini-enemies. Teleporters appear from nowhere.
10💀 MEGA BOSS. Plus Phantoms (mostly invisible) join every future wave. Pure chaos.
10+All enemy types can spawn. HP and speed scale up every wave. Good luck.
Pro Tips

How to Survive Longer

1
Keep MovingDon't stand still. Constantly move Pombon in a circle to shoot enemies from all angles at once.
2
Prioritize ShootersKill Shooter enemies first — they fire back at you and are the most dangerous at range.
3
Save the NukeHold onto 💥 Screen Nuke drops for boss waves or when you're surrounded. Don't waste them.
4
Splitters — Back Off!When a Splitter dies it makes 2 minis. Step back before shooting the last one.
5
Use the EdgesHerd enemies from the center toward a corner so you can mow through them in a line.
6
Watch for PhantomsPhantoms flicker visible for short bursts. Keep sweeping your cursor near where they move.
